Ford GoBike System data¶

In this project Ford GoBike System data, we take dataset one and two months on 2018 and We'll use graphs. First step, we merge one and two months dataset and take advantage of the Existing data. Second step, the univariant exploration. Third step, bivariate exploration. Fourth step, multivariate exploration. Last step, we'll put three questions and answer it.
